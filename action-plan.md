# Decision Gate Calendar

| Gate | Checkpoint | Key Question | Possible Outcomes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Day 30 | Foundation | Is product ready? Is team set up? | Advance to beta or extend build phase |
| Day 60 | First signal | Are people converting? Which messages work? | Advance to validation or rework onboarding |
| Day 90 | Commercial validation | Is the model proven? Can we lock pricing? | Advance to launch or pivot pricing/product |
| Day 180 | Scale viability | Is public launch working? Are economics healthy? | Scale up or narrow scope |
| Day 365 | Year 1 review | Did we hit target? Is there a path to break-even? | Expand or restructure |

Each gate has a founder meeting with data. Decisions documented in decision log. No gut calls.
