The Career Score — The Moat

A composite score based on CV quality, interview readiness, networking activity, skill development, and job market fit.

  • A number to obsess over: "My score went from 62 to 78 this month"
  • A signal recruiters trust: GRG prioritizes high-score candidates
  • Potential to become a standard (like IELTS for career readiness)
  • More users = better benchmarking = more valuable scores = more users
The moat is not the app. It's the data flywheel behind the score.

Product Principles

🧠 AI Teaches, User Does the Work

Not AI doing everything. AI sharpens candidate quality. The user puts in the practice.

🌍 Regional Intelligence

60-70% proprietary GRG intelligence in workflows. Generic global templates are not competition.

🔄 Daily Utility is Mandatory

If users don't open it daily, the habit loop is broken. Streaks, alerts, and practice keep them coming back.

🚫 Not a Job Board

Job boards list jobs. Zorwa makes candidates better. We improve the person, not the listing.

The key differentiator: Zorwa is a coaching platform, not a job board. The AI doesn't replace the user's effort - it directs and multiplies it. Regional intelligence from GRG's 210 recruiters is what no competitor can replicate.

Business Model

Annual-only. No free tier. Signal over vanity.

Pricing: $150/year

Why No Free Tier

  • Free-to-paid conversion: only 1.5-2.7% from installs
  • Anchors product at $0 value — "Why pay now?"
  • Attracts low-intent users who inflate vanity metrics
  • Support cost on free users burns cash for non-payers

Why Annual-Only

  • Immediate cash flow — full price upfront
  • Zero churn for 12 months — first renewal is Month 12+
  • Signals commitment — users who pay actually use the product
  • Monthly exists as a fallback, never the hero offer

Race to the Peak — Gamified Launch

Instead of a passive waitlist, the entire lifecycle is a social game. On-brand because the product IS gamification.

Precedents

  • Robinhood: 1M waitlist users pre-launch
  • Harry's: 100K signups in one week
  • Dropbox: 3,900% growth in 15 months

Mechanics

  • Referrals: +500 pts | Challenges: +200 pts
  • Social follows: +50 pts | Daily actions: +50/day
  • Leaderboard: top 100 all-time, weekly top 20, city rankings
  • Push: "You're #847. Your friend just passed you."

Tier Rewards

1,000 pts 1 month free
2,500 pts Founding badge
5,000 pts Full year free + recruiter intro
Budget: ~$7K total
vs $50K+ traditional launch campaign

Community Marketing

Career influencers like Aadhil (THE UAE HR, 120K LinkedIn followers) have audiences of exactly our target user. Frustrated, applying everywhere, getting ghosted. When someone like that recommends a tool, their audience doesn't question it. That credibility would cost thousands in ads — and people scroll past ads, not recommendations.

Execution

  • Map 20-30 career creators in UAE & Saudi
  • Give them early access — free Zorwa, let them form their own opinion
  • Selected partners get affiliate commission per signup
  • Seed WhatsApp/Telegram groups (hundreds with 500-5K members)
  • GRG recruiters as community leaders with referral links

Sequence

1. Community first → find working messages
2. Prove conversion in organic channels
3. Then scale with paid media

Never spend on paid before organic proves the message works.

Each phase has entry criteria, exit gates, and kill rules. No gut calls — data decides advancement.

Day 0-30 Foundation

Key Actions

  • Complete 6 modules end-to-end testable
  • Ship gamified waitlist with Viral Loops ($99/mo)
  • Set up measurement stack (trial, conversion, D7/D14/D30)
  • Finalize brand guidelines + bilingual logo
  • Arabic-first onboarding QA
  • Build recruiter affiliate dashboard
  • Launch career content (3-5/week)
  • Map 20-30 influencers in UAE & Saudi
  • Define CRM segments for beta push

Exit Gate

  • Product flow stable in staging
  • Instrumentation complete & verified
  • Waitlist live with 1K+ signups
  • CRM segments defined
  • Brand guidelines locked

Kill Rule

Product not testable end-to-end by Day 30 → pause all GTM, focus exclusively on product.

Day 31-90 Beta & Validation

Beta (Day 31-60)

  • CRM Wave 1 (1K candidates) → measure everything
  • Pricing Experiment: $150 vs $199 vs $249
  • Positioning Experiment: 3 message variants
  • 5-10 influencer early access partnerships
  • CRM Wave 2 (10K) with optimized messaging
  • Onboard 20 external affiliates

Validation (Day 61-90)

  • Lock annual price based on experiment data
  • Trial duration experiment (7 vs 14 days)
  • CRM Wave 3 (50K+) with proven messaging
  • Scale affiliates to 50+
  • Seed 10+ WhatsApp/Telegram communities
  • Start B2B pilot conversations (3-5 employers)

Exit Gate (Day 90)

  • Price locked with data
  • Trial-to-paid > 12%
  • D7 retention > 30%
  • D30 retention > 20%
  • CRM response rate > 15%
  • Total paid users > 500

Kill Rules

Trial-to-paid < 8% after pricing adj → pivot discussion
D30 retention < 15% → stop acquisition, fix product

Day 91-180 Public Launch

Launch Actions

  • Public launch in UAE + Riyadh (if validated)
  • 7-day "Race to the Peak" launch sprint with daily challenges
  • Activate paid media with proven messaging & strict CAC caps
  • Scale all channels simultaneously
  • Expand B2B pilots to 5-10 employers
  • Launch Zorwa Ambassador program (top 50 beta users)
  • External agency activation (events, media)

Exit Gate

  • 500+ new paid users in launch month
  • Blended CAC below ceiling
  • Retention stable or improving
  • No single-channel dependency > 60%
  • Path to break-even visible

Kill Rules

Paid CAC above ceiling 4 weeks → cut 50%, shift to organic
Total users < 1,500 at Day 180 → UAE-only, reduce team

Day 181-365 Scale

Scale Actions

  • Scale proven channels, kill underperformers
  • B2B pipeline acceleration → $200K+ pipeline
  • Year 1 retention cohort analysis & renewal preparation
  • Post-hire value tracks: career growth, salary benchmarking, alumni
  • Evaluate Year 2 expansion: supply markets, enterprise, new verticals
  • Hire specialists for proven channels

Year 1 Review Gate

  • Revenue on track for active scenario
  • Retention trend positive
  • Path to break-even within 3 months
  • Year 2 plan backed by data, not aspiration

Kill Rule

No path to operating break-even by Month 15 → renegotiate team or pivot model
